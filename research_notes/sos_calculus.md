## Abstract

The syntactic Пү-semigroup of an Пү-regular language is now constructible
[SПүS26], learnable [SПүSL26], and exploitable for definability [SПүSX26].
This paper proposes it as something more mundane and more consequential: a
*computational substrate* вҖ” the object on which the everyday operations of
an Пү-automata toolbox (Spot's, say) are performed, instead of on automata.
The calculus has three primitive moves: **align** two invariants on a
common table (a generated product, the only polynomial-priced move),
**operate** by surgery on the pair set `P` (where almost every operation
lives, almost always for free), and **reduce** to the canonical object
(re-quotient, polynomial). Complement вҖ” `2^{Оҳ(n log n)}` on
nondeterministic BГјchi automata вҖ” is one bit-flip. Equivalence вҖ” PSPACE on
automata вҖ” is byte equality. Left quotients, rootings, pair languages,
inverse substitutions: free surgeries. Classification checks that Spot
implements as constructions (stutter-invariance, safety/co-safety,
obligation, the acceptance strength actually needed) are equations on the
table, read off. The exponentials do not disappear вҖ” they concentrate,
exactly at the Пү-rational constructors (concatenation by a prefix set,
Пү-power) and existential projection, where a powerset is intrinsic. The
resulting picture is a *pay-canonicity-once* economy: entering the
calculus costs what determinization always cost; staying in it makes
everything downstream cheap, normal-formed, and certificate-producing.

## 3. The calculus

### 3.1 Three primitive moves

Every operation below factors through three moves:

1. **Align.** Put two invariants on one table: the *generated product*
   `рқ“ҳвӮҒ вҠ— рқ“ҳвӮӮ`, with class set the submonoid of `рқ’һвӮҒ Г— рқ’һвӮӮ` generated by
   `{ (О»вӮҒ(a), О»вӮӮ(a)) : a вҲҲ ОЈ }` (fresh identity adjoined), letter map
   `a вҶҰ (О»вӮҒ(a), О»вӮӮ(a))`, componentwise multiplication, and *both* pair
   sets carried along as verdict maps
   `Val_i((cвӮҒ,cвӮӮ), (dвӮҒ,dвӮӮ)) = Val_i(c_i, d_i)`. Size at most `nвӮҒВ·nвӮӮ`,
   often far less вҖ” only the generated part exists. One alignment serves
   all subsequent operations on the pair.
2. **Operate.** Surgery on pair sets over the fixed table. This is where
   the operations live, and the catalog of В§3.2 is the point: almost all
   of them are `O(|P|)` or one scan.
3. **Reduce.** Re-canonicalize: quotient the table by the congruence its
   own `Val` induces [SПүS26, В§5]. Polynomial; yields *the* syntactic
   object of the result. Reduction is the calculus's normal form вҖ” the
   move automata do not have (minimal deterministic Пү-automata do not
   exist; simplification is heuristic and model-bound).

The slogan: **align is the only product-priced move; operate is free;
reduce is the normal form.** An operation is expensive exactly when it
cannot be phrased as surgery on an aligned table вҖ” В§3.4 locates those.

### 3.2 The free fragment: the surgery catalog

All of the following act on a fixed table `(рқ’һ, О», M)`; each returns a
pair set on the same table, to be reduced at will. Proposition 5.11 of
[SПүSX26] (decomposition never leaves LTL) is the safety net for the whole
fragment: every result's syntactic algebra divides `M`.

- **Boolean algebra.** `PвӮҒ вҲӘ PвӮӮ`, `PвӮҒ вҲ© PвӮӮ`, `P^c` (relative to the
  linked pairs), differences, xor: the same-table languages form a
  *finite Boolean algebra*, isomorphic to the algebra of saturated pair
  sets. Complement is one flip вҖ” against `2^{Оҳ(n log n)}` for
  nondeterministic BГјchi complementation, this is the calculus's
  headline entry.
- **Rooting (left quotients).** For `c вҲҲ рқ’һВ№` define
  `P_c := { (s, e) linked : (cВ·s, e) вҲҲ P }`. Then
  `Val_{P_c}(x, d) = Val_P(cВ·x, d)`, so `L(P_c) = uвҒ»В№L` for any
  representative `u` of `c` вҖ” prefix subtraction is pair surgery. The
  rootings form a right `M`-action, `P_{cВ·d} = (P_c)_d`, so quotients
  compose as they must (`(uv)вҒ»В№L = vвҒ»В№(uвҒ»В№L)`), and the number of
  distinct rootings *is* the residual count read-off [SПүS26, Prop 4.6]:
  the residual automaton of `L`, internalized. These rootings are
  exactly the memoized class children of the extraction [SПүSX26, В§5.2],
  and Lemma 5.9 there (reach absorption) is a rooting identity.
- **Pair languages and prolongations.** Any union of pair classes is a
  language: `P' = {(s, e)}` gives "the words realizing exactly this
  pair" вҖ” the user-facing reading is *prolonging the language from one
  of its accepting behaviors*. The finest granularity of the OR-split
  combinator [SПүSX26, В§5.6], with its Wagner-ladder guard.
- **Inverse substitutions.** For `ПҖ : ОЈ' вҶ’ ОЈ` (relabeling, letter
  merging): compose `О» вҲҳ ПҖ`, same table, reduce. Inverse morphic images
  are free; Spot's `relabel` is a special case.
- **Canonical witnesses.** Every nonempty pair set carries its own
  certificate: `(s, e) вҲҲ P` yields the lasso `key(s)В·key(e)^Пү`, shortlex
  keys giving *the* canonical witness word. Emptiness is `P = вҲ…`;
  universality is `P = all linked pairs`; a witness or counterexample is
  read off in the same scan вҖ” the certificate discipline of
  [SПүSX26, В§4], available to every operation.
- **Decision procedures as scans.** On reduced objects: equivalence is
  byte equality [SПүS26, Thm 5.1]; inclusion `LвӮҒ вҠҶ LвӮӮ` after alignment is
  the pointwise test `ValвӮҒ вүӨ ValвӮӮ`, with the first failing pair
  furnishing the canonical separating lasso. Compare: PSPACE-complete on
  automata, with counterexamples needing product-emptiness runs.

### 3.3 The aligned fragment

Cross-table operations pay the alignment price `O(nвӮҒВ·nвӮӮ)` once:

- **Union / intersection / difference across tables**: align, then В§3.2.
  Note what is *absent*: no acceptance-condition surgery. BГјchi
  intersection needs degeneralization counters, union of deterministic
  models may not exist, generalized conditions need bookkeeping; here
  acceptance is a pair set and conjunction is pointwise `вҲ§` of verdicts.
- **Model-checking-shaped queries**: `LвӮҒ вҲ© LвӮӮ = вҲ…?` (Spot's
  `intersecting_word`) is align + scan of `ValвӮҒ вҲ§ ValвӮӮ` + canonical
  witness. Same product asymptotics as automata, plus the normal form
  and the certificate.

### 3.4 The exponential frontier

The calculus is honest about where powersets are intrinsic:

- **Concatenation by a prefix set (`WВ·L`) and Пү-power (`W^Пү`).** The
  Пү-rational constructors quantify existentially over a split position;
  no surgery on an aligned table expresses that вҖ” the syntactic object
  of the result can be exponentially larger (already so for syntactic
  monoids of concatenations on finite words). This is where the
  nondeterminism that automata carry natively re-enters; constructions
  exist [PP04] but cost what determinization costs. вҹЁTBD: precise
  statements; sharp examples; whether the census (see [SПүSN26]) shows
  the blowup is rare on small specimens.вҹ©
- **Existential projection (`remove_ap`).** Quantifying an atomic
  proposition away is the QPTL wall met in [SПүSX26, В§6]: a deterministic
  definitional extension is free, a genuine guess is a powerset. Spot
  pays the same, differently distributed.
- **Entry price.** Constructing `рқ“ҳ(L)` from a nondeterministic acceptor
  embeds a determinization; the calculus does not evade lower bounds, it
  *relocates* them to the entry gate. The economic claim is
  amortization: **pay canonicity once, then operate in the free
  fragment** вҖ” a specification pipeline that complements, conjoins,
  quotients, checks and re-checks pays the exponential once instead of
  at every complement. вҹЁTBD: a worked pipeline where Spot pays k times
  and the calculus once.вҹ©

### 3.5 Read-offs replace constructions

Spot answers classification queries by building automata and testing
them; on the invariant the same queries are equations:

- **Stutter invariance, one scan.**

  **Proposition 3.1.** `L` is stutter-invariant iff `О»(a)В·О»(a) = О»(a)`
  for every letter `a вҲҲ ОЈ`.

  *Proof sketch.* (вҮ’) `uВ·aВ·v` and `uВ·aВ·aВ·v` are stutter-equivalent in
  every context of both Arnold shapes, so `[a] = [aВ·a]` in the syntactic
  quotient. (вҮҗ) With every letter class idempotent, any finite word
  folds to the class of its destuttered form; for an Пү-word, choose
  Ramsey cut points at letter-change positions (the eventually-constant
  tail is handled directly), so a factorization of `Оұ` maps blockwise to
  one of its destuttered core with the same classes вҖ” same pair, same
  verdict. вҹЁTBD: full proof; the eventually-constant corner written
  out.вҹ© Spot's check builds and compares closure automata; here it is
  `|ОЈ|` table lookups.

- **The safetyвҖ“progress ladder** (safety, co-safety, obligation,
  recurrence, persistence, reactivity): closure conditions on `P`
  [SПүS26, В§7.1] вҖ” Spot's `is_safety`, `is_obligation`, вҖҰ as scans.
- **Acceptance strength needed** (Spot's parity/Rabin-index style
  queries): the acceptance-index read-off [SПүS26, В§7.3] вҖ” a property of
  the language, not of a chosen condition.
- **Wagner degree**: the complete read-off [SПүS26, В§7.4]; every
  hierarchy query below it specializes.
- **LTL-definability and extraction**: the aperiodicity scan, then
  [SПүSX26] for the formula or the counting-family certificate. (Spot has
  no automatonвҶ’LTL path.)
- **Hulls, conjecturally.** The safety closure of `L`, its liveness
  part, and the MannaвҖ“Pnueli decomposition `L = safety вҲ© liveness` look
  like `P`-completions along the ladder characterizations вҖ” surgery plus
  reduction, plausibly polynomial. вҹЁTBD: work out the safety-hull pair
  set; prove or refute polynomiality; this may deserve its own
  section вҖ” the temporal hierarchy as a lattice of hulls on one table.вҹ©

**Exit constructions.** The calculus should end where the consumer
needs: to an NBA of size polynomial in `|рқ’һ|` via the classical
`вӢғ L_sВ·(L_e)^Пү` decomposition over accepting pairs [PP04] вҹЁTBD: exact
size; determinized exitsвҹ©; to LTL via [SПүSX26] when aperiodic; to the
witness/certificate formats of [SПүSX26, В§4] always.

**What cannot be simulated.** Anything needing branching semantics
(games, synthesis) вҖ” the invariant is a linear-time object. And
succinctness: `рқ“ҳ(L)` can be exponentially larger than a good
nondeterministic presentation; the census [SПүSN26] measures how often
canonicity actually costs. The honest positioning: the calculus is not a
back-end for one-shot translations; it is the substrate for pipelines
that *keep* a language and work on it.
